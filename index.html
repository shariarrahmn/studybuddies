Shariar Rahman founded Study Buddies in mid-2020 as a YouTube channel. Largely self-sponsored in the beginning, Study Buddies did not initially have a website and solely reached students through educational infographics. It started creating video tutorials for Mathematics and English and eventually started taking live classes on Facebook. Study Buddies went on to cover the entire academic syllabus from Class 1 to Class 12, university admission subjects covering topics from public and private university examinations and extensive software and skills training.
